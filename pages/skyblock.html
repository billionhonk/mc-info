Hypixel Skyblock

Hypixel Skyblock is the most popular minigame on the massively successful Hypixel server (mc.hypixel.net). It takes the simple Minecraft minigame of Skyblock, a small floating island with minimal resources, and transforms it into an MMORPG with various skills, areas, monsters, NPCs, gear, and events. Game-related information can be found on the Hypixel Skyblock Wiki.

Dungeons is a combat-focused aspect of Skyblock, where up to 5 players select one of 5 classes and enter a randomly generated assortment of rooms with treasure, monsters, and bosses. Both stats and monsters are buffed in Dungeons, but players can obtain materials to create and upgrade powerful equipment that further increases Dungeons stats.

The Catacombs is a Dungeon containing 7 floors, each with stronger monsters and drops than the last. Wither Armor is obtained on the 7th floor and requires a Floor 7 completion to wear, but is used to create 4 variations, which are the best armor in the game. Due to this, it is also one of the most expensive sets, often leading players to use lower-level or budget armor sets. Shadow Assassin armor is a cheaper and comparable set for the Berserk class.
